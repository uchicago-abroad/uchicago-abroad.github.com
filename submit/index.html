Would you like to share your thoughts on your study abroad program?

Please forward your study abroad evaluation to uchicago.abroad@gmail.com. Please include in your email the location, the name of the program, and the quarter/year you attended.

We will then post your study abroad evaluation on the website. In doing so, we will make sure to remove your name and email address from the body of the evaluation.
